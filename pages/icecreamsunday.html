Ice Cream Sunday Recipe

This is the recipe for an ice cream sunday.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Ice Cream
  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Chocolate Sauce
  • Cherries
  • Nuts
  • Whipped Cream

Directions

  1. Put ice cream in the bowl
  2. Put nuts on top
  3. Put whipped cream on top
  4. Drizzle chocolate sauce over whipped cream
  5. Place a cherry on top
