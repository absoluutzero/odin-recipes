Hamburger Recipe

This is the recipe for a hamburger.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Hamburger bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup
  • Slice of Cheese

Directions

  1. Form the ground beef into a patty and grill it
  2. Spread ketchup on each bun
  3. Put the burger patty onto the bun
  4. Put the cheese on top
  5. Put the lettuce and tomato on top then close bun
