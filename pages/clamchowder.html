Clam Chowder Recipe

This is the recipe for clam chowder.

Ingredients

  • Clams
  • Heavy cream
  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • onion

Directions

  1. Boil potatoes until cooked
  2. Mix into pot with heavy cream and clams
  3. Cook onions in skillet then mix into soup pot
  4. Simmer for an hour
  5. Salt to taste and serve
